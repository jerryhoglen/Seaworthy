How it works

Step 1. Free Consultation

  • Our consultations are 100% free and we would to meet face to face to talk about your ideas and goals.
  • We know people are busy and not always available to meet face to face so we can setup video chats or email exchanges to help serve your needs.
  • We are available all over Long Island as well as New York City.
  • We will spend the time listening to your ideas and helping to build out the right plan that fits your needs.

Step 2. Design process and approval

  • After our initial consultation our designers will build out wireframes and designs that match your ideas.
  • We will then determine overall costs and timelines.
  • Next we will submit our ideas to you for approval.
  • After approval we will take a deposit for the project.
  • Finally the design will be submitted to our developers.

Step 3. Development of services

  • Our team of developers will begin to bring your designs to life.
  • We will add you to our company Slack account and provide you with progress updates.
  • Once the product is completed we will submit it for final approval.

Step 4. Final approval and launch

  • We will meet again to deliver the final product.
  • After signing off on the product we will accept the remaining balance for our services.
  • Your design will then be launced based on the decisions we have agreed on.
  • We will always be available to help with any questions you may have.
